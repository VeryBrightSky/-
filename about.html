About The Crown Report

The Crown Report is an independent review site covering hair loss treatments. We read the labels, verify the prices, and check the claims against published research, because most hair loss content online is written by the brands themselves.

Affiliate disclosure

This site contains affiliate links. If you click a link to a brand like Happy Head and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This is how the site is funded. Affiliate relationships never change our verdicts: we recommend cheaper competitors wherever they're the better fit, and you'll find us doing exactly that in our comparisons.

Medical disclaimer

We are not physicians, and nothing on this site is medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Finasteride, dutasteride, and minoxidil are medications with real risks, including sexual side effects and dangers in pregnancy. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider before starting, stopping, or combining treatments. Statements about products have not been evaluated by the FDA.

Editorial standards

Every review follows the same protocol: pricing pulled from the brand's live checkout, ingredient concentrations from product labels, and health claims checked against published research and FDA approval status. We name what is off-label. We recommend competitors when they fit better. Every page shows its last verification date, and we re-verify pricing on a rolling basis.

Contact

Corrections, questions, or partnership inquiries: vitowebpro@gmail.com. We answer correction requests first.

Accuracy

Prices and formulations are verified at the time of writing and re-checked periodically, but brands change pricing without notice. Always confirm current prices, ingredients, and terms on the brand's own site before purchasing. Found an error? Contact us and we'll fix it.
