Real timelines from real readers beat any marketing photo. Tell us what you used and what happened. · reader-supported, partner links marked (details)

The most useful thing on any hair loss site isn't a review, it's another person's honest timeline: what they used, when the shed hit, when photos started showing a difference, and whether they'd do it again. If you're 3+ months into any treatment, covered on this site or not, we want your story. Good, bad, or "nothing happened", null results help readers too.

What to include

Treatment
What you used (brand + actives if you know them) and for how long
Timeline
Month-by-month: shed? first changes? where you are now
Verdict
Would you recommend it? What surprised you?
Photos (optional)
Before/after in similar lighting, faces cropped if you prefer

Reviewed by a human before anything is published. No account needed.

How we publish reader stories

Every submission is read by a human. We publish with your permission only, using your first name or a handle, and we'll confirm the final version with you before it goes live. We edit for length and clarity, never for verdict, critical stories run as written. We don't publish medical claims ("cured my X"), identifying details you didn't approve, or anything that reads like an ad. If you were compensated by any brand, tell us and we'll disclose it or decline the story.

Why bother? Your 6-month timeline will be read by hundreds of people sitting exactly where you sat at month 0, deciding whether to start and whether to quit during the shed. It's the highest-value content on the internet for them, and only you can write it.
