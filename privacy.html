1. Summary in plain words

The Crown Report ("we," "this site") is a static informational website. There are no accounts, no login, no comment system, and no forms that store data on our servers. The interactive tools run entirely inside your browser. The short version: we collect almost nothing, we never see your health information, and the main data events on this site happen when you choose to click an outbound link or email us. The sections below spell out each case precisely.

3. On-site tools (quiz, calculator, simulator)

The 60-second quiz, cost calculator, and regrowth timeline run entirely client-side, in your browser. Your answers and selections are processed in page memory only: they are not sent to us, not saved to your device, and not shared with anyone. Closing the tab erases them.

4. Cookies and similar technologies

This site does not set advertising or tracking cookies of its own. If we later enable a privacy-respecting analytics service or an advertising partner, this policy and the table above will be updated first, and where required by law you will see a consent or opt-out mechanism before any such technology loads. Third-party sites you visit from our links set their own cookies, described next.

5. Affiliate links, what happens when you click

Links marked as affiliate or partner links route through tracking URLs operated by affiliate networks (for example, the network that manages a brand's partner program). Clicking one is a data event on their systems: the network and the destination brand may record the click, set attribution cookies in your browser, and associate a later purchase with this site so we can be paid a commission. We receive aggregate reporting (clicks, conversions, commission amounts) and never receive your name, payment details, or anything you submit to the brand, including medical questionnaires. Those are governed by the brand's and network's own privacy policies, which we encourage you to read before purchasing.

6. Third-party services we rely on

Hosting/CDN (serves the pages and keeps standard security logs), Google Fonts (loads the site's typefaces; Google may log the font request, including your IP address), affiliate networks (as described in section 5), and, if enabled in the future, an analytics provider. We choose vendors with published privacy commitments, and we don't grant any third party the right to use data from this site for their own marketing.

7. Sale and sharing of personal information

We do not sell personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA). Because affiliate networks pay this site commissions, we treat outbound affiliate clicks transparently: the click is initiated by you, is disclosed at the link, and involves no personal information supplied by us. We have no data-broker relationships.

8. Your privacy rights

If you are in the EU/EEA or UK (GDPR/UK GDPR): you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority. Our legal bases are legitimate interest (operating and securing an informational website) and consent (any future analytics or advertising requiring it).

If you are a resident of California or another US state with a privacy law (including Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Texas, and others): you have the right to know what personal information is collected, to access it, to correct it, to delete it, to opt out of sale or sharing, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. Given how little this site collects, most requests will return "nothing held," but we honor them all the same.

To exercise any right, use either contact method in section 14. We verify requests by replying to the email address involved, respond within the legally required window (45 days for US state requests, one month under GDPR), and will tell you plainly if an exemption applies.

9. Global Privacy Control and Do Not Track

Because this site sets no tracking cookies and sells no data, there is nothing for an opt-out signal to switch off today. Nonetheless: if we ever enable technologies covered by opt-out rights, we will treat browser-based Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals as a valid opt-out request, as California law requires, and display confirmation that the signal was honored.

10. Data retention

Retention periods are listed per-category in the table in section 2. The principle throughout: nothing is kept longer than the purpose requires, and correspondence is deleted on request unless a legal obligation requires keeping it.

11. Children's privacy

This site discusses prescription medications and is intended for adults 18 and over. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 (or under 13 for COPPA purposes). If you believe a minor has sent us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

12. Security and international transfers

The site is served over HTTPS. Because it is static and stores no user database, the attack surface for your data is minimal by design. Our hosting and font providers may process technical data (like IP addresses in logs) on servers in the United States; where EU/UK data is involved, those providers rely on recognized transfer mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses or the EU to US Data Privacy Framework.

13. Changes to this policy

We review this policy at least every 12 months and whenever our actual practices change, for example, before enabling analytics, advertising, or a story-submission form. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above, and significant ones will be noted on the site. The policy always describes what we actually do, not boilerplate.

14. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: email vitowebpro@gmail.com with the subject "Privacy request," or use the contact details on the About page. Either method works; no account is required to make a request.

This policy describes an informational website and is provided for transparency; it is not legal advice to any other party. If our practices and this text ever diverge, tell us, accuracy here is a commitment, not decoration.
