How to read this

The gap between routes is real, but so is the reason people pay it. The DIY generic route is the proven floor: oral finasteride plus 5% minoxidil. Every dollar above that buys either convenience (Hims/Keeps handle the prescription and shipping), strength (Happy Head compounds 8% minoxidil and dutasteride, above anything OTC), or side-effect strategy (topical delivery to reduce systemic exposure). If you're paying premium prices for basic ingredients, this calculator will make that obvious. If you're paying for something the cheap route genuinely can't give you, it'll show you the cost of that choice honestly.
