The protocol

Every treatment on this site does one of three jobs

Understand the jobs and every product page on the internet gets easier to judge.

01

Block DHT

Finasteride and dutasteride slow the hormone that shrinks follicles. This is the defense. Without it, everything else fights uphill.

02

Restart growth

Minoxidil pushes resting follicles back into their growth cycle. Concentration matters: 5% is the pharmacy ceiling, 8% needs a prescription.

03

Hold the line

Results take 3 to 6 months and last only as long as treatment continues. The best protocol is the one you can afford for years.
